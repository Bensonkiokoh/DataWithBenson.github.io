Mobile Sales Insights with Power Bi

This Power BI project analyzes mobile phone sales across different models, regions, and customer segments. It digs into what devices are selling the most, where demand is strongest, and how customer preferences vary by location and demographics. The goal is to turn raw sales data into clear, actionable insights supporting better decisions in inventory planning, regional marketing strategies, and customer targeting. The dashboard includes dynamic visuals that highlight best-sellers, sales trends over time, regional performance, and segment behavior, making it easy to explore patterns and identify opportunities for growth.

Northwind Business Insights

This project dives into the Northwind database using SQL to understand how the business performs across products, customers, employees, and operations. I looked at what’s selling the most, when revenue peaks, who’s handling the most orders, and how quickly orders get fulfilled. The whole analysis is built around real business questions each one answered with a focused SQL query and clear insight. From spotting top products and revenue driving categories to identifying delays in shipping and trends over time, the goal is simple: turn raw data into useful answers that actually help make better decisions.
